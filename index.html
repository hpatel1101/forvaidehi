How does RSVP work?

Guests search by name, see their party together, choose attending or not attending for each guest, and then submit to a Google Form that stores responses in your linked Google Sheet.

Can one person RSVP for the whole household?

Yes. Once a party is found, every guest in that party appears together on one RSVP screen.

Where do responses go?
